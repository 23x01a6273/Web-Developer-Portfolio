Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex applications may take 2-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your pricing structure?

I offer both project-based and hourly pricing options. Project-based quotes are provided after understanding your requirements, while my hourly rate is competitive for the industry. I'm happy to discuss pricing details during our consultation.

Do you offer maintenance after project completion?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and running smoothly. These can be tailored to your specific needs and budget.

What is your design process?

My design process involves understanding your goals, researching your industry and audience, creating wireframes and mockups, iterating based on your feedback, and finally implementing the approved design with clean, efficient code.
